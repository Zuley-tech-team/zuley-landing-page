ORDERING & PAYMENT:
Q1: How do I place an order?
A: Browse our products, select your item, customize if desired, and add to 
cart. Follow the checkout process to complete your order. We accept credit/
debit cards, UPI, net banking, and wallets.

Q2: Do you accept international orders?
A: Currently, we ship within India only. International shipping coming soon!

Q3: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital 
wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay).

Q4: Is my payment information secure?
A: Yes! We use industry-standard SSL encryption. We never store your card 
detailsâ€”all payments are processed through secure payment gateways.

PERSONALIZATION:
Q5: How does personalization work?
A: Select your product, click "Customize," enter your text, choose a font, 
and preview your engraving. We'll engrave it exactly as shown in the preview.

Q6: Can I change my engraving after ordering?
A: Engraving changes can be made within 2 hours of order placement. After 
that, production begins and changes aren't possible. Contact us immediately 
if you need to make changes.

Q7: What if I make a spelling mistake?
A: Please review your engraving carefully before checkout. We engrave exactly 
what you enter. If there's an error on our end, we'll remake it free of charge.

SHIPPING & DELIVERY:
Q8: How long does delivery take?
A: Standard products: 5-7 business days. Personalized products: 7-9 business 
days (includes 2 days for engraving). Express delivery available.

Q9: Do you ship to my city?
A: We ship across India to all serviceable PIN codes. Enter your PIN code 
at checkout to confirm delivery availability.

Q10: How can I track my order?
A: You'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS once your order ships. 
Use this to track your package in real-time.

RETURNS & REFUNDS:
Q11: Can I return a personalized product?
A: Personalized items cannot be returned unless there's a manufacturing defect 
or error on our part. Non-personalized items can be returned within 30 days.

Q12: What is your return policy?
A: Non-personalized items: 30-day return window. Personalized items: Only if 
defective or incorrect. See our Returns Policy for full details.

PRODUCT CARE:
Q13: How do I clean my silver product?
A: Wipe with the included polishing cloth. For heavier tarnish, use silver 
cleaning solution. See our Craftsmanship page for detailed care instructions.

Q14: Will my silver tarnish?
A: Silver naturally tarnishes when exposed to air and moisture. This is normal 
and easily reversed with polishing. Store in the provided anti-tarnish pouch 
to minimize tarnishing.

CORPORATE GIFTING:
Q15: Do you offer bulk discounts?
A: Yes! Orders of 10+ pieces qualify for volume discounts. Contact our 
corporate team for a custom quote.
